## Vision & Mission

### Our Purpose

- To bridge the gap between the community and the masjid.
- To digitize masjid services, making them easily accessible to all.
- To empower musallees with real-time updates, schedules, and announcements.
- To facilitate sadaqah and donations for sustainable masjid operations.
- To bring the ummah together through education, connectivity, and shared
  services.

### How We Achieve This

- A Centralized Digital Platform - All masjid & madrasa services in one place.
- Real-time Updates - Prayer times, events, and announcements at your
  fingertips.
- Community Engagement - Verified musallees contribute & maintain masjid data.
- Islamic Marketplace & Services - Everything a Muslim needs in one ecosystem.

## Features & Roadmap

Our roadmap is divided into our **Committed Phase (MVP)** and future *
*Conceptual Phases**.

### Phase 1: Committed MVP

This is our current pilot launch development focus, Alhamdulillah.

1. **Landing Page:** A visually appealing and informative public-facing page
   that clearly articulates the InMasjid App vision, mission, features, and
   benefits. It will be mobile-responsive and serve as the primary hub for
   community engagement and onboarding.

2. **Masajid Listing and Search:** A core function for the digital onboarding of
   Masjid profiles. This includes essential, searchable information like
   `name, address, geo-location, prayer times, contact details, updates, etc.`

3. **Musallee Onboarding and Follow:** A structured user registration system
   with a clear hierarchy (e.g., Imam, Super Musallee, Musallee, Guest) to
   ensure data integrity. It allows users to follow their local Masjid and
   receive a personalized feed of relevant information.

4. **Sadaqah Jariyah Platform:** A secure, transparent, and **extremely trust,
   audit, and metric-centric** donation system. It will enable direct donations
   for operational costs and crowdfunded, large-scale projects (e.g., new
   construction), while providing users with history, receipts, and impact
   tracking.

## What's Next? - Conceptual Phases

Following the successful launch of the MVP, we plan to explore these exciting
new feature categories, In Sha Allah.

### Market

* **Madrasa Listing and Search:** Similar to the Masjid directory, this feature
  allows for the digital onboarding of Madrasas and Islamic Centers with
  searchable details like `classes offered, curriculum, staff, etc.` Approved
  Madrasas can also be listed on the Sadaqah Jariyah Platform.
* **Muslim Store (Marketplace):** A curated e-commerce marketplace for essential
  Islamic goods, including books, tasbeeh, and both inner and outer modest
  attire.
* **Masjid Library:** A feature to digitize Masjid libraries, creating
  searchable catalogs and potentially offering digital borrowing options for the
  community.

### Media

* **QGram (Video):** A dedicated, curated, and distraction-free Islamic video
  streaming platform for authentic Quranic recitations, Tafseer, Hadith & Zikr.
  It will be strictly Qur'an and Sunnah compliant, including a "wudu mode".
* **Halal Chords (Audio):** A library of Shariah-compliant background music,
  nasheeds, and poems without haram instruments for Muslim content creators.
* **Islamic Radio (24/7):** A live and on-demand Islamic audio streaming service
  featuring 24/7 Qur'an recitations, halaal music, podcasts, and more.
* **Live Masjid Streams:** A feature to allow Masajids to stream events like
  Jumu'ah khutbahs, halaqas, and special lectures live to the community.
* **Hifz Companion App:** A dedicated tool or integrated feature-set to help
  Huffaz and students track Hifz progress, set revision reminders, and use tools
  like auto-replay to aid memorization.

### Finance

* **Halal Financing and Banking:** A revolutionary financial platform to
  facilitate halaal, interest-free financing options, aiming to transform the
  banking sector in an Islam-friendly manner.
* **Halal Stock Screener:** A financial tool to help users screen stocks for
  Shariah compliance, making Halal investing transparent, accessible, and a
  viable entry into the stock market ecosystem.
* **Ulema Fund:** A dedicated charitable fund within the platform to financially
  support Islamic scholars and their work.

### Community

* **Nikah Connect:** A trusted, private, and Halal matrimony service with
  verified profiles to facilitate local matchmaking within Islamic guidelines.
* **Ulema Monitored AI Daawah Bots:** An AI-powered knowledge hub, controlled
  and verified by Islamic experts, to provide guidance and answers on all
  aspects of life and deen.
* **Masjid Events Calendar:** A centralized, interactive calendar for users to
  discover and track all upcoming events, classes, and gatherings at their local
  Masajid.
* **Open Masjid Days and Community Connect:** A platform to help Masajids
  organize, promote, and manage "Open Day" events to welcome the wider
  non-Muslim community and foster understanding.
